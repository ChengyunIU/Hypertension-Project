Prevalence of Hypertension from 2011 to 2018


The purpose of this project is to present the prvalence of hypertension in recent years. The data of this project comes from the National Health and Nutrition Examination Surveys (NHANES) Individuals were considered as hypertension if their systolic blood pressure > 130mm Hg or their diastolic blood pressure > 80mm Hg. Demographic variables such as gender, age, race, education level and income level are selected from the original demographic data set. The age was treated as a categorical variables with 4 categories (20-39, 40-59, 60-79, and 80+).
